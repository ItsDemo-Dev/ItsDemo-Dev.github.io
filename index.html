RANK SYSTEM

RECRUIT

0 points

Responsibilities

  • Learn crew structure and rules
  • Participate in basic training
  • Follow leadership directives
  • Attend crew meetings

Requirements

  • Join the Discord server
  • Complete introduction
  • Read crew guidelines

Role in Crew

New members learning the ways of the revolution. Recruits are the foundation upon which future leaders are built.

TRAINEE

20 points

Responsibilities

  • Complete advanced training modules
  • Assist senior members
  • Participate in crew activities
  • Develop specialized skills

Requirements

  • Complete basic training
  • Earn 20 contribution points
  • Show active participation

Role in Crew

Members in training, learning advanced tactics and crew operations. Trainees begin to take on more responsibilities.

CADET

Responsibilities

  • Lead small squad operations
  • Mentor new recruits
  • Execute strategic missions
  • Contribute to crew planning

Requirements

  • Complete advanced training
  • Earn 50 contribution points
  • Demonstrate leadership potential

Role in Crew

Junior officers beginning to lead and make tactical decisions. Cadets are the future commanders of the revolution.

CORPORAL

Responsibilities

  • Coordinate multi-person operations
  • Train lower ranks
  • Implement crew strategies

  • Complete leadership course

Experienced leaders who command respect and authority. Corporals are essential to crew operations and training.

SERGEANT
